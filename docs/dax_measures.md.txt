# DAX Measures (Road Accidents)

## Total Accidents
- Definition: Count of accidents
- Example: Total Accidents = COUNT(Road[accident_id])

## Accidents by Severity
- Definition: Count filtered by severity category

## Severity Rate (%)
- Definition: Severity count / Total accidents
- Example: Rate = DIVIDE([Fatal Accidents], [Total Accidents])

## Trend Measures (optional)
- Accidents by Month, Accidents by Day of Week (if Date table exists)
